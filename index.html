    • Team420 Nethunter Apps

    The core functionality of Nethunter lies in the Nethunter apps. Unfortunately, the original Nethunter app does look really outdated and has some bugs here and there. With our custom made Team420 Nethunter apps (Team420 Terminal & Team420 Nethunter) we are trying to fix some bugs, which haven’t been addressed by the official Nethunter Team yet. We are also patching some features into the app, which weren’t present or functional before. At the same time, we gave the app an optical upgrade, to finally make thatol’ boy look modern.

    Team420 Kernels

    To be able to use Kali Linux Nethunter at all, the bare minimum is to have a custom Kernel with activated configs and included patches. That’s why we are offering separate Team420 Kernel zips.
